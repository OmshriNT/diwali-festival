A festival of lights.

Diwali is a festival of lights mainly celebrated by Hindus. The festival usually lasts five days and celebrated during the Hindu Lunisolar month Kartika. One of the most popular festivals of Hinduism, Diwali symbolizes the spiritual "victory of light over darkness, good over evil, and knowledge over ignorance".

A festival which brings good fortune and health by lighting your home with Diyas (lamps/laterns) with a positive mind.
